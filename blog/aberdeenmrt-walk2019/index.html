    Aberdeen MRT - Sponsored Walk 2019

    By Stewart Ainslie on 17.05.2019

    Walking miles in their boots

    Aberdeen Mountain Rescue Team (Aberdeen MRT) are a life saving charity who's volunteers operate in the north East of Scotland.

    On June 8th 2019 we will foolishly disregard our own company name and join them for their Sponsored Walk.

    In the meantime you might see us making a bit of noise for them on social media…

    Sign up here: https://amrt.org.uk/Sponsored-Walks
